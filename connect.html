Manual import

No Fortnox connection? Fill in a spreadsheet and upload it here.

  1. Download the template — it already has your reporting lines:
  2. Open it in Excel, fill in the Amount column (one row per line and month), save.
  3. Upload the saved file below — you'll see a preview before anything is written.

Format: Reporting Line; Month (1–24); Amount — separated by ;, comma, or tab. Pasting rows into the box works too.
